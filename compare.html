Recommendations

Based on the analytics shown above and as depicted in the graph, Bitcoin has demonstrated superior short-term performance, particularly during its initial boom. However, it is important to note that Bitcoin's volatility makes it a less stable investment option.

On the other hand, the S&P 500 has shown consistent growth over the past century, recovering from all its drawdowns. The index is dynamic, with underperforming companies being replaced by stronger ones, ensuring that only the top 500 companies contribute to its performance at any given time.

If you have a significant amount of capital that you do not need in the near future and are unsure where to invest, I recommend considering the S&P 500. While it may not offer the rapid growth potential of Bitcoin, it provides a more stable and steady return over the long term.
